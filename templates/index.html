About Smart Krishi

To create an integrated platform to solve operational problems in agriculture and make farms more efficient using machine learning and deep learning. Agriculture is one of the important occupations practiced in India. It is the broadest economic sector and plays a most important role in the overall development of the country. More than 60% of the land in the country is used for agriculture in order to meet the growing needs of 1.3 billion people. Adopting new agriculture technologies is very important.

Objectives, Goals and scope of Smart Krishi

• To provide an integrated platform to solve problems related to crop yield, soil fertility, irrigation cycles, health, inventory, and financing of agricultural operations.
• To enable farmers to make data-driven decisions.
• To apply scientific approaches to increase the profitability of small farms.
• To maintain the health of crops and prevent diseases and crop failures.
• To keep track of crop life-cycle and growth.
• To maintain a consistent inventory of the various supplements and paraphernalia required to grow crops.
• To renew the fertility and nutrients prevalent in the soil by predicting fertilizers and types of crops to be grown.
• To predict prices and increase accessibility to funding.
